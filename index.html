My name is Ashia and I like to build things that move -- rockets, cars, whatever's next. My passion mostly lies in spacecraft engineering, and I've been inspired by NASA and SpaceX for my previous projects. As much as I love learning new things, I also enjoy sharing my knowledge with others. I have been a math tutor for 5 years now, and I love getting to know my students and form meaningful connections with the future generation. I hope to use my degrees in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering to give back to the community that made me so that everyone can have the same opportunities that I did to pursue their dreams in the stars.
